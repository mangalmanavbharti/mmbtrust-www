### Founding Inspiration

Dr. Shashi Mangal is the primary visionary and founding inspiration behind Mangal Manav Bharti Trust. Her decades-long work towards building a just, educated, and equitable society has laid the foundational vision of this institution.

#### Early Life and Inspiration

Dr. Shashi Mangal spent her early years deeply engaged in observing and contributing to social and educational efforts. Her personal motivation has consistently been to ensure that knowledge and empowerment reach those who have remained outside the mainstream.

For her, education is not limited to formal learning, but a powerful tool to bring meaningful and positive change in the lives of underserved communities.

#### Social Work Since the 1990s

Since the 1990s, Dr. Mangal has actively led multiple grassroots initiatives focused on social upliftment. Her work spans women empowerment, local education initiatives, and community development programs.

She has worked consistently at the ground level for decades, guided purely by a commitment to public welfare rather than recognition or personal gain.

#### Perspective on Education and Society

Her core belief is that a truly healthy society can only be built when access to knowledge and education is equal for all.

She envisions the development of knowledge institutions and platforms where no individual is excluded on the basis of caste, religion, language, or economic background.

She also considers digital literacy and intellectual dialogue as essential pillars for strengthening society’s collective intellectual foundation.

#### Role in Establishing the Trust

As the founding inspiration, Dr. Shashi Mangal has defined the core values and direction of the Trust.

Her vision transformed the Trust from being just an administrative entity into a meaningful platform for public service and intellectual development.
