## Introduction

Mangal Manav Bharti Trust is an institution dedicated to public good. Built on principles of equity and knowledge sharing, the Trust functions as a centralized mechanism for fostering educational and intellectual growth.

## Vision and Objectives

The goal of the Trust is to take education and research to every section of society. Our core objectives include establishing knowledge centers, promoting literature, making digital education accessible, and creating a strong intellectual environment for the exchange of ideas.

## Governance

This institution works purely for public welfare. Our work is free from any commercial profit or self-interest. Service to society is rendered without discrimination of caste, religion, language, or sect, with a special focus on the upliftment of marginalized and deprived sections of society.
